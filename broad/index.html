Broad City
Title Sequence
2016
 

Overview

Broad City chronicles the misadventures of Abby and Alana, two young women struggling to navigate through the chaos of New York City. The original intro is simple, abstract, and changes every episode. While being short and sweet, I felt that it seemed to not communicate any of the themes of the show: feminism, sexuality, or drugs. Using the existing countdown music as a base, I briefly sketched some storyboards.

 
 

Sketches

 
 
 
 
 
 
 

Modelling

Starting with subdivided primitives, I began sculpting a dildo, making sure to include all necessary details. Once I was satisfied with the geometry, I spline-wrapped the model to a bezier curve in the shape of numbers.

 
 
 
 

Lighting & Textures

For each scene, the dildos would alternate between four different 360° panoramas of New York City, each capturing a unique time and place. I experimented with various materials, but ultimately settled on a one with a subtle gradient because it created a fake subsurface-scattering effect (which my laptop normally wouldn’t be able to handle).

 
 
 
 

Cinematography

I varied close up and extreme close-up shots, quickly cutting between the two to reflect the song and New York City. The dildos were put into cloners and spline-wrapped for the numbered sections, animating their offset. Other shots were cloned dildos with their rotation and posistion randomly affected.
