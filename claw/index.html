Claw of Shame
Motion Design
2017
 

Overview

Nathan For You is a docu-reality comedy show in which the host, Nathan Fielder proposes intentionally terrible business ideas to struggling businesses. In this special episode, Nathan proves that he can take risks too by putting himself in a dangerous scenario.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 

Preliminary Sketches

I opted to use masonite to create the woodblock type, which proved to be inexpensive and effective at holding ink. Each block was made of 4 layers of masonite—three layers for the base and the fourth layer for the raised letterform. To ensure proper registration when assembling each woodblock, I used the surrounding masonite as a guide, clamping it to the base layers.

 

Modelling

The magic behind the show is the unfathomable level of cringe that is produced in each episode. The people on the show aren’t actors, yet Nathan Fielder is able to manipulate them into awkward situations without breaking character. I wanted to communicate this otherworldly theme by avoiding realism and using a punchy colour scheme.
