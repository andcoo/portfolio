Miscellaneous Work
A collection of smaller things.
2015-Present
 

Sentry Mech

An exercise in hard surface modelling with Fusion360. Heavily inspired by Legohaulic's racer mechs, which I loved as a kid.

 

Uncanny Valley Film Fest

In the pursuit of computer-generated realism, people can start to appear unnatural. This is the central theme in this promotion for the fictional Uncanny Valley Film Fest.

 

Claw of Shame

Nathan For You is a docu-reality comedy show in which the host, Nathan Fielder proposes intentionally terrible business ideas to struggling businesses. In this special episode, Nathan proves that he can take risks too by putting himself in a dangerous scenario. Warning: although this show is satircal, you may not wish to watch this if you have trouble with potentially sensitive topics.

 

Motion Design Exercises

Made for YSDN4003: Type in Motion.

1. Combining the words "octane", "dangerous", and "energy".
2. Animated type projected onto lasercut transparent acrylic.

 

Portfolio Workshop Invitations

Thaddeus Miya and I collaborated on a series of invitations for portfolio workshops run for prospective students. The workshop was created to raise funds for my year's graduation showcase, so we used highlighters to keep printing costs low.

 

Collapse Kinetic Type

An animation experiment inspired by Seiho's track COLLAPSE (demoware).

 

Do The Astral Plane Type Poster

Using a dissection and combination of 4 typefaces, this poster was inspired by Flying Lotus' song, representing a realm of immaterial existence.
